Volunteering

“Let me win, but if I cannot win, let me be brave in the attempt.” This is the Special Olympics motto I live and breathe. Outside of school and swimming, I take time every week to volunteer as a coach and Unified Partner for the Porter County Special Olympics Swim Team. I devote 2 hours every Sunday, and when I can, an hour on Wednesdays, to help improve stroke techniques and overall fitness.

Not only a coach, but I also enjoy chaperoning Special Olympic swimmers to full-day meets, and even the full- weekend Special Olympics Summer Games.

Apart from volunteering with Special Olympics I enjoy taking part in local food drives and pantries, helping to donate and deliver food to the needy.

I also give back to my school community by peer tutoring students to help for standardized tests or just helping understand certain topics in math, english, or science.
